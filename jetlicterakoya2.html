Project overview

JETLIC Terakoya is a place where children can learn not only programming but also computer knowledge online for free

We rent out PCs and teach beginners 1: 1 one-on-one so that as many children as possible can learn.

There is no teacher. We JETLIC members will study together, and we can ask our favorite children.
